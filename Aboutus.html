About us!

We are a group of entrepreneurs nourishing the world with love. Our story starts at the London School of Economics, where we met during our Master programme. A group of intelligent and powerful women aiming to make a difference. We engaged during our coding class sponsored by Code First Girls to start this life-chaning project. We currently help finance the work of other women in Australia, Indonesia and Brazil. We are hosted in London where we combine forces with other 56 partner organisations. We thank Code Fist Girl for this opportunity in special Melenie Schatynski, Sophie Koonin and Paul Cuthbertson who taught us our first coding baby steps and triggered a a global alliance of love.
